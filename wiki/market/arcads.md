# Arcads — Competitor Profile

## TL;DR
Arcads specializes in realistic AI actors for UGC-style ads. Strong actor quality, but it's actor-centric rather than system-centric. No brief engine, no performance loop. Pricing not publicly listed.

---

## Profile

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Website | arcads.ai |
| Core strength | Realistic AI actors, UGC-feel ads |
| Primary weakness | Actor-centric, not system-centric; no strategy layer |
| Pricing | Not public as of April 2026 |

---

## What They Do

- Large library of AI actors with high visual realism
- UGC/talking-head style ads — very authentic feel
- DTC-focused (product pages, Shopify stores)
- Hook-based scripting tools

---

## What They Don't Do

- ❌ No creative brief engine
- ❌ No multi-format generation (T2V, product demo, etc.)
- ❌ No performance feedback loop
- ❌ Not built for agencies (multi-client workspaces)

---

## Qvora vs. Arcads

Arcads is a strong tool for one format (UGC/talking-head). Qvora is a system that produces multiple formats from a single URL, with strategy as the entry point. They're partially overlapping on V2V output, but Qvora uses HeyGen v3 for avatar quality anyway.

> 🔍 Gap: Arcads pricing is not public. Could be comparable to Creatify or higher. Check their blog/announcements.
