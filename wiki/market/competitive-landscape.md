# Competitive Landscape

## TL;DR
Creatify is the primary competitor and market validator. Arcads, HeyGen, and AdCreative.ai are in the space but not direct threats. Qvora's unique position is the **strategy layer before generation** + the **closed performance loop** (V2) — neither of which Creatify has.

---

## Comparison Table

| | **Qvora** | **Creatify** | **Arcads** | **HeyGen** | **AdCreative.ai** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Core strength** | Brief engine + full loop | Scale, ad launcher, analytics | Realistic AI actors | Avatar quality, translation | Ad image + copy |
| **Strategy layer** | ✅ GPT-4o angles + hooks | ❌ Template-first | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |

| **Pricing entry** | $99/mo | $33/mo | Not public | $29/mo | ~$29/mo |

---

## Qvora's Differentiated Position

**What no competitor does:**
1. Generate creative angles and hooks *before* video generation (the Brief engine)
2. Apply those angles consistently across all variants
3. Close the loop from performance data back to brief regeneration (V2)

**The wedge:** Qvora starts with *strategy*, not templates. Every other tool starts with a template or a format. Qvora starts with "what angle is most likely to convert, and why."

---

## Positioning Statement

> *For performance marketing agencies managing $50K–$500K/month in ad spend, Qvora is the only creative production system that starts with a data-informed creative strategy and closes the loop from ad performance back to brief regeneration — unlike Creatify, which starts with templates and requires manual strategy overhead.*
