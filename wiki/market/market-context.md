# Market Context — Creative Velocity & CPM Trends

## TL;DR
CPMs are rising on every major platform. The only lever performance teams can pull without increasing spend is creative quality and velocity. No tool fully closes the brief → production → performance → next iteration loop.

---

## CPM Landscape (2026)

| Platform | Average CPM | Trend |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram | $9.46 | ↑ Rising |
| TikTok | $4 – $9 | ↑ Rising |
| YouTube | $4 – $5 | ↑ Rising |
| Pinterest | $4.67 | ↑ Rising |

*Source: Hootsuite Social Media Advertising Report, 2026*

**Implication:** The same ad budget reaches fewer people every quarter. Creative quality is the only free variable left.

---

## The Creative Velocity Problem

**Current state:**
- 3–5 day cycle: brief → approved ad set
- 20–40 creative variants tested per month; teams need 80+
- Performance data lives in one tool, creative in another
- Teams run dead creatives because fatigue detection is manual
- No tool closes the full loop autonomously

**The gap Qvora addresses:**
> No existing product fully closes the **brief → production → performance feedback → next iteration** loop autonomously.

---

## Platform Case Study

**PureGym (Meta / Instagram Reels):**
- Ran lo-fi, native-style Reels designed to blend into the feed
- Result: **5.6x increase in Thruplays** vs. polished ads
- Faster and cheaper to produce
- Key insight: **authenticity + native format beats production value for performance ads**

*Source: Hootsuite / Meta, 2026*

**Implication for Qvora:** UGC-style and VOX ads should be prioritized in generation defaults. Polished/cinematic is a secondary template.

## Why Now (Structural Tailwinds)

1. **CPM inflation** is forcing performance teams to compete on creative output, not just budget.
2. **Short-form video is now the primary paid social format** — TikTok, Reels, Shorts have displaced static image.
3. **AI video quality crossed a viability threshold in 2024–2025** — FAL.AI, Sora, Runway, Kling produce ad-quality output.
4. **Agency consolidation** — more brands hired fewer agencies but demand more output from them.
5. **Attribution is harder** post-iOS 14/17 — creative differentiation is one of the last clear levers.
