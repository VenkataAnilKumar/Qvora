# AdCreative.ai — Competitor Profile

## TL;DR
AdCreative.ai generates static ad images and copy. Minimal video capability. Not a direct threat in the video-first ad space. Pricing ~$29/mo entry.

---

## Profile

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Core strength | Ad image generation, copy variants |
| Primary weakness | Minimal video; no brief engine |
| Pricing | ~$29/mo |

---

## Why They're Relevant

Static image ads are a parallel market. Some performance buyers use both image and video tools. Qvora could eventually encroach on their territory by adding static creative generation, but this is not V1 scope.

---

## Overlap with Qvora

Minimal. Qvora is video-first; AdCreative is image-first. Users of AdCreative who run video campaigns likely use a separate video tool — that's the gap Qvora fills.
