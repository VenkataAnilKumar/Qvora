## Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Pillars

**1. Direct**
> Say what you mean. Cut the padding.
- ✅ *"Paste a URL. Get 10 ads."*
- ❌ *"Our innovative platform leverages artificial intelligence to streamline your creative production workflow."*

**2. Confident**
> Own the category. Don't hedge.
- ✅ *"Your best-performing ad hasn't been made yet. Qvora makes it today."*
- ❌ *"We think Qvora might help you improve your ad creative."*

**3. Provocative**
> Challenge the status quo. Make people feel their current process is broken.
- ✅ *"You spent 3 days on that ad. It got 0.4% CTR. There's a better way."*
- ❌ *"We understand creative production can sometimes be time-consuming."*

**4. Human**
> Smart but never cold. The AI works behind the scenes — the voice stays warm.
- ✅ *"Your creative team is brilliant. Stop making them do boring work."*
- ❌ *"Our neural network processes your input data to generate optimized output assets."*

```
──────────────────────────────────────────────

  ████████  Convert Green    #00E87A
            Used only for success states, CTAs, performance wins

  ████████  Signal Red       #FF3D3D
            Used only for fatigue alerts, warnings, urgency

  ████████  Data Blue        #2E9CFF
            Used only for analytics, charts, performance data
```

### Color Personality
- **Black + Violet** = primary brand expression (hero sections, logo, marketing)
- **White** = breathing room and readability
- **Green** = conversion, wins, positive performance signals
- **Accents** = functional only, never decorative

## Brand Do's and Don'ts

| ✅ DO | ❌ DON'T |
|---|---|
| Lead with outcomes (CTR, CPA, ROAS) | Lead with features (AI, models, tech stack) |
| Use short, punchy sentences | Write long paragraphs |
| Show real performance numbers | Make vague claims without data |
| Speak to the pain before the solution | Jump straight to product features |
| Use "Qvora" as a proper noun (capital Q) | Write "qvora" or "QVORA" |
| Own the category confidently | Apologize for being AI-generated |
